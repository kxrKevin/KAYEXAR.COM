In a few short years, KAYEXAR(Kevin Li & Roberto Kuehr) has established themselves as one of the top performing EDM acts to come out of London, Ontario, Canada.

After the initial success of their 2021 and 2022 releases, which garnered hundreds of thousands of streams, the duo entered 2023 with high expectations that were undeniably met. Releases such as "On My Own," "Want It," and "Caffeine" were featured on popular radio stations in Europe and the US/Canada. This very success caught the attention of large music distributors such as AWAL and local Canadian record labels offering promotion services.

With now an accumulated 1.4 Million Streams and no intentions of slowing down, Kayexar’s mission statement into 2026 is to begin performing live sets and release “INFRARED”: their upcoming 20 track album two years in the making. This ambitious project aims to experiment with new sounds, genres/subgenres and break the existing boundaries of commercial electronic dance music.
